Inception

Inception centers around a name called Dom Cobb who is a highly-skilled infiltrator of the human mind during dreams and extracts
corporate secrets at the behest of others. Due to trauma of the past, Dom is no longer able to return to his children and thus seeks to
take one final job to come back to his children.
